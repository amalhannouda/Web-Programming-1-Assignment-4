Legzira beach

Legzira beach is in the south of Morocco, about 10 kilometers from Sidi Ifni city. this beach is characterized by the golden sand, the crystal clear and calm sea, and especially the great ocher cliffs which excite. The sound of sea breaking on the rocks, the nuances of the rays that fade at sunset and cover everything with a fiery red, will leave you speechless.

It is an opportunity to admire the unique landscape of a huge beach that stretches as far as the eye can see over the Atlantic Ocean, hidden in the hollow of a magnificent cliff with ocher-colored boulders.

Quemado beach

Al Hoceima city is nicknamed the "Pearl of the Mediterranean". it is surrounded by serie of marvelous beaches. Quemado beach is one of the most popular. The water is crystal clear and the sand will offer you a unique delicacy. Nearby, in addition to bars and restaurants where you can taste the typical Moroccan cuisine, you will find many shops in which you can choose the beautiful memories of your holidays.

Las cuevas beach

A very beautiful beach in the surroundings of the city Asilah. a beautiful tourist resort, popular for its white buildings. nickname "Paradise beach". It is a pearl set in the sand and the rocks whose access is not easy, because it can only be done on foot, with a beautiful walk in the greenery.
